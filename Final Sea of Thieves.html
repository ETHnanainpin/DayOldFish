Sea of Thieves

Sea of Thieves is a multiplayer game set in a Pirate setting. You can play by yourself or up to 4 friends and there are different types pf ships that correspond to the amount of players you have with you. Along with the dfferent types of ships there are different organizations you can join. You can become an emissary for each of them and each is different.
